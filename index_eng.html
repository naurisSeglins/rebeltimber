About

Rebel Timber was founded in 2020. We started out as providers of woodworking services and manufacturing individual orders. However our main idea was to create our own catalogue of products that we would manufacture in our plant and sell to customers ready to go. Rebel Timber manufacture transportable and modular saunas and also garden furniture from solid wood with built-in extras.
